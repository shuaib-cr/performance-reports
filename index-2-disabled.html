Section 03 — Audience Guide

Intelligence By Role

Each team has a unique intelligence need. Below is a summary of what critical signals each function should be extracting — and requesting — from these reports.

📡
Media Buying Teams
Google Ads · Meta · Paid Social
  • Cost-per-donation (CPD) by platform and cause
  • ROAS benchmarks and trend lines
  • Audience fatigue signals by creative set
  • Budget pacing vs. actual donation velocity
  • Top-performing ad formats and hooks
📱
Owned Media Teams
SMS · Email · WhatsApp
  • Open rates, click rates, conversion rates by list
  • Revenue attributed to owned channel campaigns
  • Optimal send windows and frequency analysis
  • Segment performance (recurring vs. lapsed donors)
  • WhatsApp broadcast response and opt-out rates
🏛️
Executive & Leadership
CEO · COO · Board
  • Total revenue vs. target and YoY comparison
  • Cause portfolio health and priority ranking
  • Donor lifetime value and retention rate trends
  • Strategic risk flags and opportunity windows
  • Resource allocation recommendations
🤝
Influencer Managing Teams
Creator Partnerships · Ambassadors
  • Revenue and donation volume attributed to each partner
  • Audience overlap and unique reach per influencer
  • Engagement-to-conversion rates by creator
  • Content themes driving highest donation intent
  • Partnership ROI and renewal recommendations
📋
Account Managing Teams
Donor Relations · CRM
  • High-value donor segment behaviour and giving patterns
  • Lapsed donor identification and reactivation triggers
  • Recurring giving upgrade opportunities
  • Cause affinity mapping per donor segment
  • Touchpoint history and communication effectiveness
⚡
Critical Decision Makers
Non-Profit & Agency Leadership
  • Cross-channel attribution to understand true acquisition cost
  • Campaign-level profitability (net revenue after ad spend)
  • Predictive indicators for scaling or pausing campaigns
  • Competitive benchmarks and sector context
  • Go / no-go signals for new cause or market entry
Don't see your role above? Use the report request form below to describe your intelligence need — our team will configure a bespoke output tailored to your decision-making context.
